Asian Lettuce Wraps

Description

These Asian lettuce wraps are a delicious and healthy meal that is perfect for a light lunch or dinner. They are made with ground chicken, fresh vegetables, and a flavorful sauce that is both sweet and savory. The lettuce leaves add a refreshing crunch, making these wraps a satisfying and nutritious option. These Asian lettuce wraps are easy to make and can be customized with your favorite ingredients. They are sure to be a hit with your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup hoisin sauce
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 can water chestnuts, drained and chopped
  • 1/4 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1 head butter lettuce, leaves separated
  • Sriracha sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the ground chicken and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger and cook for 1-2 minutes, until fragrant.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the hoisin sauce, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and sesame oil.
  5. Pour the sauce over the chicken mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Add the water chestnuts and green onions and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Remove from heat and spoon the chicken mixture into the lettuce leaves.
  8. Drizzle with Sriracha sauce, if desired.
  9. Enjoy!
